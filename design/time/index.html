Evolution of Craft Beer: Throughout the Ages


Craft Beer has a long history in the United States. I took important events from the early 1900s and placed those events on a timeline display. Both displays exhibit magnitude, and the distances between events.

How I emulated my models:
Consistent with my color model, I used a navy blue background, and orange and teal foreground colors. I attempted to have a balance of orange and teal to not clutter the displays. The body text has elements taken from my base model, with an attempt to estabish a visual heirarcy with the text size and weights.
